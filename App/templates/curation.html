Elite18X Impact

Elite18X is an Israeli foundation dedicated to transforming IDF reservists into entrepreneurs and business leaders — helping those who served rebuild their careers and drive Israel's economy forward.

Through my connection with Mike Silberg, whom I met through the Hoffman Kofman Foundation, I was invited to curate wine for their mentors' event — a room that included former IDF Chief of Staff Aviv Kochavi. I sourced a Malbec 2024 from the Galilee, and the remarkable volunteer team at Elite18X created a custom label specially crafted for the event — a fitting tribute to an extraordinary group of people.
